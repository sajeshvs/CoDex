Transaction Process Guide

Phase 1: Property Listing

  1. Sign Form A with property owner
  2. Verify title deed at DLD portal
  3. Obtain Trakheesi permit for advertising
  4. Create marketing materials with QR code
  5. List on approved portals (Bayut, Property Finder)
  6. Follow three-broker rule (maximum 3 brokers)

Phase 2: Negotiation & Contract

  1. Present offers formally in writing
  2. Document all negotiations for transparency
  3. Prepare Form F (MoU) once price agreed
  4. Review terms with both parties
  5. Collect signatures from buyer and seller
  6. Secure deposit (typically 10% of purchase price)

Phase 3: Due Diligence

  1. Request NOC from developer
  2. Verify service charge clearance
  3. Check mortgage status if applicable
  4. Review all documentation
  5. Confirm community rules and regulations
  6. Processing time: 3-7 working days

Phase 4: Transfer & Completion

  1. Meet at DLD Trustee Office (all parties present)
  2. Submit required documents (title deed, NOCs, IDs)
  3. Buyer pays 4% DLD fee plus admin charges
  4. Process manager's cheques
  5. New title deed issued to buyer
  6. Commission released to agents
